Theoretical Chemistry

Theoretical Chemistry broadly deals with modeling physical and chemical phenomena from first principles of interacting systems. In our group we deal with the most fundamental microscopic regime of interacting electrons and nuclei. We build and use models describing these interactions with quantum mechanics to describe bonding, reactivity and spectroscopy. Our group is dynamic and flexible and we are forever expanding our horizons. Please feel free to contact us to know about our most recent endeavors. Reaction Dynamics


We are currently engaged in fitting potential energy surfaces to carry out reaction dynamics both classically and quantum mechanically. One of our long-term aims is to describe astrochemical areactions under extreme extraterrestrial conditions.
